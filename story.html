Our Story

Azeroth best monburger is founded months before the initial release of The Burning Crusade Expansion.
Our forefathers were bored adventurers who already cleared every content in Vanilla Wow and didn't like the pvp grind.
What started as a side project soon became big enough to have a presence in every major city.

At first our products made only local ingridients from Elwynn Forest(Our Murloc patty is still the most succesful item on the shop)
As our influence grow, so did the selection of choices for the burger meats!From wolf, murloc, bear, spider and gnoll
we arrived to the more exotic choices like naga,demon,satyr, various dinosaur meats,dragon etc.

Along the way, we had to make a few compromises, such as: Cease to use half naked females boosting our traffic(remind you we started in Goldshire)
Taking off Gnome and Goblin meat from the menus along with the Worgen
Dismantle the whole Black Market we built around importing ingridients etc
But our business survived every obstacles got in the way,and still going strong to this day

For the last note, we like to thank everyone involved in growing the shop to what is it today:
Our staff, our dear customers, our partners in business
and last but not least to the millions of slauthered creature to give us their meat for the greater good.
Hope to see your around, have a fantastic time with us!
